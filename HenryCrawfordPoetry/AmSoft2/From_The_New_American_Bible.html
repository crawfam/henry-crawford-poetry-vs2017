From the New American Bible

19:27 And it came to pass that the word went out across
the valley to develop a broadband path for the Lord
and all His disciples to enter into the Kingdom of Heaven.

19:28 And yea, there came a camel into the laboratories
of the valley to be shorn and set down upon a well prepared
table to be mildly sedated and euthanized.

19:29 And when the animal hath passed into darkness
there appeared the washed and scrubbed Nano-technicians.
And lo, the Lord hath prepared His Lab with great care.

19:30 Now verily, saith the Lord, "How hath a startup
to profit without VC funding?" So the Lord did obtain
securitized instruments of all manner in His name
and in the names of all His disciples and  yea, the Lord
doth deliver the  funds offshore to the island of Cayman.

19:31 And it came to pass that a call went out
to the engines of search to find a needle most pure.
And such a needle was found in the lands of the East
and it did travel across a great sea in a ship of containers.

19:32 And the disciples took the needle to the Lord
and verily, the Lord and His disciples descended
upon the Lab where the camel had been rendered asunder.

19:33 And the head of the camel and the flesh of the camel
and yea even the hairs of the camel were caused to pass
through the eye of the needle.

19:34 And the Lord and  his disciples did much rejoice
and the Lord prophesied, verily I say unto you in the end
of days, think thee first of thyself, for the first shall be first
and the last shall always be last.
