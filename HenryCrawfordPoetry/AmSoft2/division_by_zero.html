Division by Zero

Fear is [translucent] as the thin [reductive] sun
uncovers a warren of slant suburban roofs
as it [pokes slothful] round [the fenders] of SUVs
parked [white] and [black] in prearranged slots
the way coffins [are bought and sold] with extra
[holes] for coffee cups as the external day comes
rollicking to life in each [family wigwam] where
the Eskimos of dawn are packed and patted down
[equipped] and renumbered in [loose hoodies]
barely a [breakfast bowl] un-spooned in sync
with the unsung songs of digital clocks pacing
the [unsuspected] eavesdropper [the] sleep walker
[the] dream slacker [the] old leashed couple
waving hapless to [the] afternoon shoppers
clutching silk purses spun out of world markets
prepared by the skeletal fingers [of the poor]
in the last of the Holy Lands still exploding
on screens of waxed horror as the null day
counts down the dusk hours [in odds and evens]
like calendar pages in a [Hollywood] script
and just getting ahead is becoming harder
amid so many [neighbors] marching horizontally
in [standard deviation] tightening the cables
[rolling out] the [bandwidth] coming home
to a factory of [endless open doors] before
parking the devices [filtering the windows]
[looking in] on the appliances until [it's time]
to gaze up [to be narcotized] by the whirl
of a ceiling fan [counting backwards from zero]
shaken by the stillness [breathing until breathless]
