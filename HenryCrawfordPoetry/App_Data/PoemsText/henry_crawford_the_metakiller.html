The Metakiller

let's say this is a poem
a refrigerator magnet poem
[killer] [inside] [standing] [behind]
but it's centered in a camera shot
slowly panning a suburban
kitchen

let's also say the kitchen scene
is just an animation being watched
by a family of [blue human frogs]
sitting around a [retro TV]
comically drawn with rabbit ears
on AdultSwim

but now let's say the cartoon
is jusy playing in the background
in a house of college students
in a 90's style [slasher] film
while a killer lurks outside
watching through a window

and let's say one of the [students]
decides to leave the room saying
["let me take a look"]

so let's say she's walking alone
into the kitchen and there's
an extreme [close up] on the
killer's shoes as the girl begins
reading a one line poem stuck
on a refrigerator door


First published in Scryptic Magazine of Alternative Art, 2018.
