The Officers of the National Rifle Association

an Interactive Poem by Henry Crawford

Carolyn Meadows is the President of the NRA [ Alberto
Romero is the father of a dead 6 year old boy ] the First
Vice President is Charles L. Cotton [ the child's name was
Stephen ] Willes Lee is Second Vice President of the NRA
[ he was killed by an assault-type rifle ] General Counsel
is John Frazer [ Romero said, "I lost my son" ] Chris W. Cox
was the Executive Director [ "he had his whole life to live" ]
for the Institute for Legislative Action [ "and he was only 6" ]
the CEO of the NRA is Wayne LaPierre [ "there's nothing
I really can do" ] Joseph De Bergalis, Jr. is Executive Director,
General Operations [ "besides try to be with him" ] NRA
officers were elected [ "until I can put him in his resting
spot" ] unanimously and unopposed.
